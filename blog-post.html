Quick Smoothie Bowl

by Patrice Breakfast Meal 213 Upvotes

Vegan, Gluten-Free, Vegetarian, & Dairy-Free

"Your favorite fruits, 1 blender, and 5 minutes. That's it."

Smoothie Bowl Section

  • 1 heaping cup organic frozen mixed berries
  • 1 small ripe banana (sliced and frozen)
  • 2-3 Tbsp light coconut or almond milk (plus more as needed)
  • 1 scoop plain or vanilla protein powder of choice* (optional)

Toppings Section

  • 1 Tbsp shredded unsweetened coconut
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 Tbsp hemp seeds
  • Granola (optional)
  • Fruit (optional)

Steps:

  1. Add frozen fruit to a blender and blend on low
  2. Scrape down the sides until you have soft serve
  3. Then add your favorite toppings!

Try a spoonful coconut flakes, hemp seeds, and chia seeds for added nutrients, fiber, and protein! 😊
